Works with CJ Affiliate

Your CJ Affiliate products, live inside your WordPress posts

Your CJ Affiliate catalogs hold more products than you could ever place by hand, and most of them never make it into a post. MyFeeds makes the catalog searchable inside the WordPress block editor: pick a product, publish, and the card keeps its price and stock current on its own.

From feed to published post, in three steps

Get your feed link

In your CJ account, create a Product Export subscription (Account, then Subscriptions). CJ delivers the file on a schedule via SFTP, an HTTPS link or email; hand MyFeeds the file or link you receive.

Paste it into MyFeeds

The importer reads CSV, tab- or pipe-delimited and XML exports, usually gzip-compressed, sniffs the structure and maps the fields on its own. A few minutes later your products are searchable inside WordPress.

Pick products in the editor and publish

Search your catalogue right inside the post, click, and the card is in. A nightly sync keeps every card on every post current; products that disappear from the feed get flagged so old posts stop pointing at 404s.

What MyFeeds handles for CJ Affiliate feeds

  • ✓ Reads CSV, tab- or pipe-delimited and XML exports, usually gzip-compressed; unusual columns are matched by the smart mapper
  • ✓ Affiliate deep links are used exactly as your feed delivers them; your tracking stays intact
  • ✓ Every click carries an id via CJ's sid parameter, so commissions can be matched back to the exact post and product in the MyFeeds analytics dashboard (Pro)
  • ✓ Nightly sync updates price, stock and sale badges on every published card
  • ✓ Everything is stored in your own WordPress database, fully self-hosted

Clicks and commissions

On paid plans, MyFeeds tags every outgoing click through CJ's sid parameter and pulls your commissions back into WordPress, so you see which post and which product actually earned. Clicks are counted server-side; ad blockers don't punch holes into your numbers.

Common questions

Does MyFeeds work with CJ Affiliate?

Yes. If CJ Affiliate gives you a product feed file or URL for a program you have joined, MyFeeds imports it, maps the fields automatically and turns it into a searchable catalogue inside the WordPress block editor.

Do CJ product catalogs need manual field mapping?

Usually not. The smart mapper reads the header row, recognises common CJ column names and picks the price, image and deep link columns on its own. You can override any single field afterwards.

Do I have to pay to get started?

No. The free version lives on WordPress.org and covers one feed, the smart search, the grid layout, manual plus nightly sync. Upgrade only when you outgrow it.

Where does my data live?

On your own WordPress site, in your own database. The plugin doesn't proxy product images, doesn't ping a third-party server when a page loads, and doesn't send anything about your visitors anywhere.
