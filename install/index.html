Install MyFeeds

Two paths. Free is one click from the WordPress.org directory. Pro is uploaded after you start your trial — five steps, no command line, never touches code.

Free

Install from WordPress.org

The free version covers one feed, smart search, the grid block, manual and nightly sync. No license key required — install and go.

MyFeeds — Affiliate Product Feed Manager Version 1.0.3 · WordPress 5.8+ · PHP 7.4+ · Free on WordPress.org
View on WordPress.org
1

Open your WordPress admin

Log in to your WordPress site. You should land on the Dashboard, the main admin page with the sidebar on the left.

2

Go to Plugins → Add New Plugin

In the left sidebar, hover over Plugins and click Add New Plugin. You'll see the WordPress.org plugin directory's search bar at the top of the page.

3

Search for "MyFeeds"

Type MyFeeds into the search box. The plugin titled MyFeeds — Affiliate Product Feed Manager appears in the results. Click Install Now, then Activate once installation completes.

Prefer to install from a ZIP? Click View on WordPress.org above to download the plugin ZIP, then in WP Admin choose Plugins → Add New Plugin → Upload Plugin and select the file. Use this path if your site can't reach wordpress.org directly.
4

Add your first feed

Click MyFeeds in the sidebar, then Add Feed. Paste your product feed URL, give it a name, and let the import run in the background.

Outgrown the free plan? When one feed isn't enough, or you want the card to match your brand, or you'd like a full shop on your domain, start a free trial on myfeeds.site. Pro runs for 7 days, Business for 14.
Pro

Activate Pro or Business after your trial starts

Started a trial on the pricing page? Freemius emails you a download link the moment your trial is created. Five steps, two minutes, and your unlimited feeds, carousel, analytics and design editor light up — same plugin, paid features unlocked.

1

Open your purchase email from Freemius

Subject line is "Thank you for your purchase" or "Your MyFeeds Pro/Business plan". Inside is a personalised download link for the latest plugin ZIP and your license key.

Don't see it? Check the spam folder, then sign in to users.freemius.com. Your purchases page has a Download button next to every active license.
2

Download the Pro plugin ZIP

Click the download link. You'll get a file named something like myfeeds-affiliate-feed-manager-pro.zip. Save it somewhere you can find it — Downloads folder is fine.

3

Deactivate the free plugin (if installed)

In WP Admin go to Plugins, find MyFeeds — Affiliate Product Feed Manager and click Deactivate. Your feeds, mappings and posts stay intact — they live in the database, not the plugin folder.

No need to delete it. Deactivating is enough. The Pro plugin runs alongside it without conflict, and you can keep the free version installed if you want a safety net.
4

Upload the ZIP to WordPress

Still in Plugins, click Add New Plugin at the top, then Upload Plugin. Choose the ZIP from step 2, click Install Now, then Activate Plugin.

5

Activate your license

On the first activation, Freemius shows a screen asking for your license key. Paste the key from the purchase email, click Agree & Activate License. Pro (or Business) features go live immediately — unlimited feeds, carousel block, analytics dashboard, design editor, all unlocked.

License limits. One license activates on one site by default. Need more sites? Upgrade to a multi-site license from your Freemius dashboard, or contact support@myfeeds.site.
