Common questions

How long do I keep earning per customer?

As long as the customer's subscription stays active. Monthly plans, annual plans, every renewal. If they cancel, the trail ends; if they re-subscribe later, the new period counts again.

What's the commission percentage?

30% recurring as the default. For creators with strong reach in the WordPress or affiliate space, we'll talk individual terms.

Do I need a website?

No. Social channels, newsletters, podcasts and email all count. Your coupon code works even where links can't.

Can I recommend the free version too?

Yes. The free plugin lives on WordPress.org. If a reader installs the free version and later upgrades to Pro or Business, the upgrade still attributes to you for the cookie window after the click.

How quickly do you approve?

Usually within a few days. We read every application personally.

What's the cookie window?

60 days. The reader clicks your link or uses your code today; they can take the next two months to decide and you still get the credit.

When and how do I get paid?

Monthly through Freemius once your balance crosses the payout threshold. PayPal and bank transfer are the standard options.

Can I work with other affiliate plugins at the same time?

Yes, no exclusivity. Recommend whatever genuinely helps your audience.
